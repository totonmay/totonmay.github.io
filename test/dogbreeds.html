Pembroke Welsh Corgi

Corgis are affectionate, cute, and friendly dogs that have strong athletic capabilities. They weigh 27 to 30 pounds and come in a range of colors from red, sable, fawn, black, tan, and white.

  1. Family life
    • Good with children
    • Affectionate with family
    • Good with other dogs
  2. Physical
    • Shedding level high
    • Low drooling level
    • Coat grooming moderate
  3. Social
    • Open to strangers
    • Very playful
    • Very protective

Corgi Care

Health Grooming Exercise
